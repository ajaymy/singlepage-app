What We Do

Our Purpose

OLIMIN works towards making access to clean power, universal and ubiquitous. OLIMIN focuses on developing and deploying solutions and products to minimise common man's reliance on traditional sources of power and the grid. The focus areas are roof top systems (both standalone and grid-tied), agricultural water pumps and micro-grids for remote communities infrastructure installations that are not connected to the power grid. OLIMIN will apply the scalability of solar energy to bring the power to generate and use electricity to the last marginalised citizen. OLIMIN intends to bring energy self-sufficiency to the people, empowering them to focus all their energy and daily activities on improving their quality of life.

Our Goal

OLIMIN's goal is to make sunlight the universal source of electricity.

The Power of the Sun

At OLIMIN,we understand that the one source of energy for our Planet Earth that is abundant, inexhaustible, clean and is FREE, is the Sun. It is the one resource that does not deplete when you consume, and the consumption process itself does not impact the environment in any manner. Solar Energy gives us the freedom to choose to harvest where we need, when we need, how much we need. No other energy resource offers such scalability and geographical extents. One may choose to have a small lantern to illuminate a book, or to electrify a community, or the entire planet.
