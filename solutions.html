mySUN is a solar photovoltaic (PV) system that has electricity-generating modules mounted on the rooftop of a residential or a commercial building. The system converts sun light (PV) into usable electricity. This electricity can then run all the electric appliances in your home, office or factory and any excess generated can be transmitted back to the power grid.

For the system to operate, the mySUN system must have grid power available and connected. Advanced state-of-the-art electronics of the system maximise the amount of sun light that is converted to electricity and delivered for consumption or transmission to the grid.

OLIMIN Off-Grid Solutions for remote locations without a grid connection

 

OLIMIN’s myGRID brings access-to-electricity closer home to people and businesses without a mains grid connection, at a reasonable cost. myGRID brings you a pre-configured, self-contained, tried and tested system built from durable, high-quality components. myGRID allows you to connect a diesel generator, a wind mill and even an AC mains, if you have one.

myGRID uses energy from the solar array to charge the batteries, where energy will be stored until its needed. The array then tops up the batteries as the power is used. The system can also support additional loads to those listed when connected to a generator. In addition, if a generator is connected and the batteries are depleted and no solar power is available, the system will send a start signal to the generator to generate power and charge the batteries.
